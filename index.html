What is Capital Transit?

Capital Transit is an Amazon Echo skill that allows you to get the latest train arrivals and service alerts for the Washington DC Metro Transit System. You can install it by telling your Echo "Alexa, enable Capital Transit."

Example Interactions

Here are some things you can try with the Capital Transit Skill:

Alexa, ask Capital Transit when the next train arrives at Metro Center
Alexa, ask Capital Transit for departures from Metro Center
Alexa, ask Capital Transit are there any delays
